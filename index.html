Who We Are

Welcome to Owen-Goodall Quantity Surveyors, based in Christchurch, New Zealand. We specialise in providing expert cost management and advisory services to help our clients achieve optimal project outcomes. Our dedicated team is committed to delivering excellence in every aspect of quantity surveying.

Our Services

Cost Planning & Management

Feasibility cost appraisals, value engineering, tender estimates, and cost reporting for optimal project outcomes.

Contract Administration

Procurement, pre-contract negotiations, tendering, and contractor appointments to streamline your projects.

Insurance Services

Reinstatement valuations, repair estimates, and expert support during insurance claim negotiations.

Bank Finance Reporting

Detailed project risk assessments, feasibility reviews, and drawdown reporting to secure financial milestones.
