Who We Are

At Owen-Goodall Quantity Surveyors (OGQS), our goal is to simplify and improve the building process for our clients. We're driven by a strong, values-based culture that emphasises how we work, not just what we do. Our core values—integrity, responsibility, and accountability—guide everything we do and are deeply embedded in our team. We see ourselves as financial problem solvers within the construction industry. Through our work, we provide clarity on project costs, helping clients make informed decisions and navigate the balance between necessity and desire. Accurately predicting construction costs requires more than just spreadsheets and databases. It demands an understanding of project quality, timelines, and procurement methods. This comprehensive approach is the foundation of all our services at OGQS.
